# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Security hiring managers, SOC/DFIR/CTI leads, malware analysis teams, technical recruiters, and peers checking Edwin's public work before a conversation.

## Product Purpose
This is Edwin Chen's public portfolio and GitHub Pages site. It should make the current CV legible fast: junior malware analyst and CTI profile, reverse engineering focus, practical security tooling, and selected public repositories. Success means a visitor understands the role fit and has enough evidence to continue to GitHub or LinkedIn.

## Brand Personality
Forensic, precise, builderly, and calm. The site should feel technical without cosplay, confident without overclaiming, and current without looking template-made.

## Anti-references
Avoid generic hacker stock aesthetics, braggy red-team language, phishing/C2 glorification, noisy repo dumps, exposed private contact details, and AI SaaS card-grid sameness.

## Design Principles
- Evidence before hype: make roles, tools, and shipped projects specific.
- Defensive framing: research and offensive concepts are presented as analysis, detection, and authorized testing.
- Repository showcase first: public work should be curated, current, and easy to scan.
- Privacy-aware: keep contact paths public-safe and avoid unnecessary personal details.
- Keep the existing identity: dark cinematic surface, serif display type, and restrained purple accents stay recognizable.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Aim for readable contrast on the dark surface, keyboard-reachable links and close actions, reduced-motion tolerance where possible, and responsive layouts that do not overflow on small screens.
